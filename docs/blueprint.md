# **App Name**: FuelTrack Pro

## Core Features:

- Fuel Type Configuration: Set fixed prices per liter for petrol and diesel.
- Sales Transaction: Record fuel sales by quantity and automatically calculate total amount based on configured fuel prices.
- Real-time Stock Tracking: Monitor and update the remaining stock levels of each fuel type in real-time.
- Daily Expense Logging: Log all daily expenses, automatically deducting these expenses from the total daily sales.
- Credit Management (Udhar): Manage customer credit accounts, subtracting credit sales and adding repayments to maintain accurate balances.
- Sales Reporting: Report for total daily sales based on configurations and input

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#1E3A8A), evoking a sense of trust, stability, and professionalism which is important in financial and management tools. This will allow the FuelTrack Pro to feel like a serious business platform.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F4F8), offering a clean and unobtrusive backdrop that highlights the important content and data.
- Accent color: Vibrant teal (#00A36C), providing contrast and attracting the eye to key actionable items.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif with a modern look suitable for headlines and body text, offering readability and a clean aesthetic.
- Use clear, professional icons that represent different fuel types, transaction types, and reporting functions. The style of the icons should be simple and monochromatic to ensure clarity.
- Maintain a clean, organized layout with a focus on data presentation. Utilize tables and charts for displaying stock levels, sales data, and expense tracking. Ensure that all key information is easily accessible and navigable.
- Subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback on user actions, such as loading new data or updating stock levels. Avoid distracting or unnecessary animations to maintain a professional feel.
